Thread Dynamics // Fasteners

Never Force a Cross-Thread

If a fastener or spark plug stops cold within the first three full rotations by hand, it is cross-threading. Forcing it cold-cuts new, offset tracks into the soft aluminum or casting, destroying the parent thread completely.

Quick Diagnostics Reference

  • Sweet, sticky smell Coolant Leak
  • Acrid, chemical bite Electrical Fire
  • Thick, blue smoke Oil Combustion
  • White, sweet cloud Head Gasket Fail
  • Heavy, black exhaust Rich Fuel Mix
